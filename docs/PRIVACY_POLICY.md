# InsureIT Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 04 July 2026  
**Last updated:** 04 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how InsureIT collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when a user accesses or uses the InsureIT website, mobile application, web portal, claim assistance platform, or related services collectively referred to as the **Platform**.

InsureIT is a commercial vehicle insurance and claim assistance platform designed to help customers, vehicle owners, claim processors, field executives, managers, and administrators manage commercial vehicle insurance support, policy records, claim cases, accident reporting, claim documents, follow-up tasks, communication, and support workflows.

By using the Platform, creating an account, submitting a claim, uploading documents, requesting insurance assistance, or communicating with us, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is intended to be read together with any applicable terms of use, consent forms, insurance proposal forms, claim declarations, service agreements, and any notices shown inside the Platform.

## 1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through:

- the InsureIT mobile application;
- the InsureIT web portal;
- the InsureIT website or landing pages;
- customer onboarding and login flows;
- policy, vehicle, and claim management features;
- claim document upload and verification workflows;
- support ticket, helpdesk, phone, email, or messaging communication;
- field executive or surveyor workflows;
- notifications, reports, analytics, and administrative dashboards.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, insurers, payment gateways, surveyors, garages, repair workshops, government portals, or other external services that may be linked from the Platform. Such third parties may have their own privacy policies and practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on the services used, the role of the user, and the information required for policy assistance, claim support, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and platform security.

### 2.1 Personal Identifiers

We may collect:

- full name;
- profile name;
- customer name;
- nominee or beneficiary details where relevant;
- government identity details when required for insurance, claim, or compliance purposes;
- customer ID, user ID, employee ID, or internal reference numbers;
- signatures, declarations, and consent records.

### 2.2 Contact Information

We may collect:

- mobile number;
- alternate phone number;
- email address;
- residential address;
- office address;
- billing address;
- emergency contact details;
- communication preferences.

### 2.3 Vehicle Information

Because InsureIT focuses on commercial vehicle insurance and claims, we may collect:

- vehicle registration number;
- chassis number;
- engine number;
- vehicle make, model, variant, and category;
- commercial vehicle type, such as truck, pickup, bus, tractor, JCB, construction equipment, goods carrier, passenger carrier, or other commercial vehicle;
- registration certificate details;
- vehicle ownership details;
- permit, fitness, tax, PUC, hypothecation, or other statutory vehicle documents;
- vehicle photographs;
- odometer or hour meter readings where applicable.

### 2.4 Policy and Insurance Information

We may collect:

- policy number;
- insurer name;
- insurance type;
- premium amount;
- policy start and end dates;
- renewal due dates;
- insured declared value and coverage details;
- nominee or insured person details;
- proposal information;
- previous policy details;
- claim history;
- renewal status;
- endorsement or cancellation information;
- insurance quotation request details.

### 2.5 Claim and Accident Information

We may collect:

- claim number;
- accident date, time, and location;
- accident description;
- FIR, police intimation, or legal documents where applicable;
- driver details and driving licence information;
- repair estimate, final bill, invoice, and payment information;
- workshop, garage, repairer, or surveyor details;
- claim status and claim timeline;
- loss assessment details;
- approval, settlement, or rejection details;
- communications related to the claim;
- photographs or videos of the vehicle, accident spot, damage, repair work, documents, or other evidence.

### 2.6 Financial and Payment Information

Where required for insurance, claim settlement, refund, payment tracking, or accounting, we may collect:

- bank account details;
- UPI or payment reference information;
- transaction IDs;
- invoice and receipt details;
- premium payment status;
- settlement amount;
- repair bill amount;
- tax or GST information where applicable.

We do not intend to store full card numbers, CVV, or payment authentication credentials. Payment processing may be handled by authorized payment gateways or financial service providers.

### 2.7 Account, Login, and Role Information

We may collect:

- login email or phone number;
- encrypted authentication identifiers;
- user role, such as customer, field executive, claim processor, manager, admin, or super admin;
- account status;
- password reset and login activity metadata;
- audit logs;
- staff assignment details;
- task allocation and action history.

Passwords are not stored in plain text by InsureIT.

### 2.8 Device, Technical, and Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

- IP address;
- device type;
- operating system;
- browser type;
- app version;
- device identifiers permitted by the operating system;
- crash logs;
- error logs;
- session information;
- pages or screens visited;
- date and time of access;
- feature usage;
- click or interaction data;
- network and performance information.

### 2.9 Location Information

The Platform may collect location information where it is necessary for features such as:

- accident reporting;
- claim spot verification;
- field executive visit tracking;
- customer support;
- nearby repair or service assistance;
- fraud prevention;
- documenting claim-related events.

Location access may require device permission. You can disable location permission from your device settings, but some features may not work correctly without location access.

### 2.10 Photos, Videos, Documents, and Multimedia

We may collect files uploaded by users, including:

- accident photographs;
- vehicle damage photographs;
- repair photographs;
- RC, policy, permit, fitness, tax, PUC, invoice, estimate, and claim documents;
- identity documents where required;
- audio, video, or signature files if used in the claim or support process.

Please do not upload unnecessary personal information or documents that are not requested by the Platform.

### 2.11 Children’s Information

The Platform is not intended for children below the age of 18. We do not knowingly create accounts for children. If child-related information is collected as part of nominee, beneficiary, dependent, or insured member details, it will be processed only with appropriate consent and only for the relevant insurance or claim purpose.

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information based on one or more of the following grounds:

- **Consent:** where you give permission for specific processing, such as receiving promotional communication or enabling device permissions.
- **Performance of services:** where processing is necessary to provide insurance assistance, claim support, account access, or other Platform services.
- **Compliance with law:** where processing is required under applicable legal, regulatory, tax, insurance, or government requirements.
- **Legitimate use:** where processing is necessary for security, fraud prevention, service improvement, customer support, business operations, audit logs, or dispute resolution, provided it does not override your rights.

- request deletion of personal information where retention is no longer required;

- raise a grievance regarding the processing of your personal information;
